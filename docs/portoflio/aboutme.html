About me

I am Gerrelle Jarina, currently studying at City Of Malabon University as a BSIT 3rd year students.

As a third-year college student, I favor a more reflective, quieter side. I like to dedicate time to artistic endeavors that let me express who I am, like writing, drawing, or taking pictures. I cherish my privacy and find serenity in solitary pursuits like idea exploration or journaling. Despite my reserve, I have an open mind, am constantly seeking personal development, and look for depth and significance in the experiences I have. My path is one of self-discovery and scheduling time for the things that are most important to me.
